Is it okay if I share a story with you about Jesus?

While Jesus was in Galilee two blind men showed up at his doorstep. The men asked Jesus to have mercy and give them sight. Jesus said “Do you believe that I am able to do this?”

When they responded "Yes, Lord." Jesus said “According to your faith be it done to you.” Their eyes immediately opened.

What's powerful about this story is that the two blind men did not question the ability of Jesus to heal their eyes. They did not worry what might happen next, they just believed that it was possible.

They did not have any doubt that Jesus could heal them. He was their only hope, so they had complete faith.

Now contrast this story to that of Matthew 17. A man approaches Jesus asking for mercy to heal his son from suffering. The man says he has tried everything, even asking the other disciples for help, but they could not heal his son.

Jesus says this is because his disciples do not have complete faith. He even calls them disobedient. Jesus thought they were deliberately not following his instructions, even though they knew the consequences.

Jesus was able to drive the demon out of the boy and heal him. The disciples asked Jesus why they were unable to heal the boy.

Jesus said it was because they had such little faith. Even if you had "faith as small as a mustard seed, you can say to this mountain, 'Move from here to there,' and it will move. Nothing will be impossible for you"

If you have been trying to unlock abundance in your life without much success, think about what Jesus said to his disciples. Even if your faith was small you could move mountains. Now imagine if you had great faith? What could you do then?
